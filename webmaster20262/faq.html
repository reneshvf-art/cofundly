FAQ & contact

Frequently asked questions.

How CoFundly works, how to use our Richmond directory, and how to reach our team for help, partnerships, or feedback.

🔍

General questions

CoFundly is a free, community-operated resource hub for residents of Richmond, Virginia. We maintain a verified directory of local services — food assistance, housing support, healthcare, mental health, jobs, legal aid, and more — to help every Richmonder find what they need quickly and without barriers.
Yes — completely. CoFundly is free for all Richmond residents. We do not charge for access, do not require account creation, and do not accept paid placements in our directory. Every resource is included on merit.
CoFundly is operated by a small team of civic-tech professionals based in Richmond, working alongside community volunteers and partner organizations. We are not a government agency, but we work closely with the City of Richmond, RPS, VCU Health, and local nonprofits to keep information accurate. Learn more about our team →
We review every listing at least quarterly and update contact details, hours, and availability as changes are reported. Organizations can notify us of changes at any time via our contact form. We flag recently verified listings so you can gauge how current the information is.
If you're in immediate crisis, go directly to our Get Help Now page. You can also call:
  • 988 — Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, 24/7
  • 911 — Life-threatening emergencies
  • (804) 972-0813 — Homeward Richmond, housing crisis intake 24/7
  • (804) 612-6126 — YWCA Richmond, domestic-violence hotline 24/7

Using the directory

Visit the Resource Directory and use the search bar to enter keywords — like "food pantry," "shelter," "tenant rights," or "therapy." You can also filter by category using the chips at the top, or narrow results by Richmond neighborhood in the sidebar.
Yes. Click the heart icon (♡) on any resource card to save it. Your saved resources are stored in your browser and persist between visits — no account needed. View them at the bottom of the directory page or via the sidebar's "My saved resources" filter.
Resources marked "Urgent" offer time-sensitive or same-day services — such as emergency food, crisis shelter, or immediate mental-health support. Use the Urgent filter in the sidebar to see only these resources.
Please contact our team and tell us which listing needs updating. Include the organization name and what appears incorrect. We aim to investigate and update listings within 48 business hours of a correction report.
It may not have been submitted yet, or it may be listed under a different name. Try searching by keyword. If you believe a legitimate Richmond organization is missing, please suggest a resource — we review every submission within 5 business days.

For organizations

Use the Suggest a Resource form. Our team reviews every submission for accuracy, community relevance, and service legitimacy before approval. Most submissions are reviewed within 3–5 business days.
No. Listing your organization is free. We don't charge for placement, and we don't accept payment to feature organizations more prominently. Our goal is to reflect the genuine resource landscape of Richmond.
Nonprofits, government agencies, community health organizations, legal aid, educational programs, and community-run services that serve Richmond residents accessibly. We don't list commercial businesses, paid-membership services, or organizations that can't demonstrate active community service.
Email updates@cofundly.org with changes. Include your organization name and the corrections. We typically process updates within 48 business hours.

Privacy & data

We use only basic, privacy-respecting analytics to understand which resources are most-viewed so we can improve coverage. No accounts are required, no personal information is collected, and saved resources live only in your browser — never on our servers.
No. Saved resources are stored in your browser's local storage. They are not sent to our servers, are never visible to CoFundly staff, and cannot be used to identify you. Clearing your browser data will remove saved resources.
None about individual visitors. We share aggregate, anonymized metrics (e.g., "your listing was viewed 120 times last month") when organizations request them. We never share search terms or contact attempts with the organizations themselves.
