Why it matters

Your support keeps this running.

Richmond residents rely on CoFundly to find food, housing, mental health support, and more. Here's exactly what donations fund.

✅

Keep listings verified

Every organization in our directory is manually reviewed and updated quarterly. Donations fund the time it takes to call, confirm hours, and remove outdated info.

📢

Expand community reach

We partner with libraries, schools, and clinics to make sure residents who don't have reliable internet access can still access CoFundly's listings in print and in person.

🛠️

Fund platform improvements

Better search, more languages, accessibility features, and mobile performance — donations keep the site improving so it serves Richmond's full diversity of needs.
