About CoFundly

Built for Richmond.
By people who call it home.

CoFundly exists because every Richmond resident — across The Fan, Carytown, Church Hill, Shockoe Bottom, Scott's Addition, Southside, and beyond — deserves to know what help is available, quickly and without barriers.

"Connecting every Richmond resident to the services, support, and opportunities they deserve." — The CoFundly mission
Our story

How CoFundly came together.

In late 2024, a coalition of Richmond social workers, RPS counselors, VCU Health case managers, and neighborhood leaders came together around one shared frustration: the safety net existed, but residents couldn't find it. Help was buried in outdated PDFs, handwritten flyers, and word-of-mouth referrals that too often fell short.

CoFundly was built to fix that. We created a single, trusted platform where any Richmonder — regardless of neighborhood, income, or background — can quickly discover the services available to them and connect with real support.

Today, CoFundly is used by residents, social workers, school counselors, RPS family liaisons, healthcare providers, and nonprofit staff across Central Virginia. We maintain verified relationships with 180+ local organizations and update listings continuously so residents never chase a dead phone line or a closed office.

What we stand for

Our core values.

Every decision — from which resources we feature to how we design this site — is guided by these principles.

🤝

Accessibility first

We build for every Richmonder. Multiple languages, screen-reader compatibility, plain language, and services that reach people where they actually are.

✅

Accuracy you can trust

Outdated information causes real harm. We verify every listing, update contact details regularly, and flag changes the moment we learn of them.

🏘️

Community, not commerce

We don't accept advertising or paid placement. Every organization we feature is here because it serves Richmond residents — nothing more.

🔒

Respect and privacy

We never share or sell user data. Residents can browse, save, and search without accounts, logins, or handing over personal information.

⚡

Speed when it matters

Many people come to CoFundly in a crisis. We prioritize clarity, simple navigation, and fast access to emergency resources above everything else.

🌱

Continuous improvement

Richmond changes. New programs launch, organizations grow, needs evolve. We're committed to keeping CoFundly useful and current, not just complete.
