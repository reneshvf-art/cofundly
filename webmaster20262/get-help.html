Step by Step

Not sure where to start?

Follow these steps to connect with the right help as quickly as possible.

  1. Identify your most urgent need

    Are you safe right now? Do you have food and shelter tonight? Prioritize immediate safety above everything else.

  2. Call the right line above

    The crisis lines and local numbers above are staffed now and can connect you to immediate resources. You don't need to have all the information ready — just call.

  3. Browse the resource directory for longer-term support

    Once immediate needs are addressed, the full resource directory has verified organizations for ongoing assistance with housing stability, healthcare, employment, and more.

  4. Ask an organization to connect you to others

    Most organizations in our directory have case managers or "navigators" who can help connect you to other services you may qualify for.
